Elara reached the ancient Moonroot Tree, guided by the map’s final symbol. With Shadow by her side, she placed the wooden charm into a hollow knot in the bark. Light broke through the canopy as whispers turned to joyous voices. The curse lifted, and warmth swept through the woods—flowers blooming where shadows once clung. When Elara returned to her village, she was no longer seen as reckless, but as the one who had healed the forest. From then on, the woods sang songs of peace to those who listened with wonder.
